Black Dog

Artist

Black Dog is poised to make a powerful impact with their energetic and heavy live sets, captivating audiences and sparking conversations. Comprising three former members of the now-defunct band Hybridreams, Black Dog was formed with a bold new vision for modern metal. Embracing their metalcore and hardcore roots with finesse and technicality, they are determined to leave a lasting impression on their local music scene.

Despite the challenge of finding a vocalist, the band didn't let it hinder their progress. Their drummer took on vocal duties, leading to the recording of their debut EP, "Slow Death Society," released in July 2022. The trio's debut single, "Slow Death," quickly gained attention, being featured on Spotify's "All New Metal" and amassing over 20k streams on the platform, as well as securing the “Nouveautés Musique Métal - Cover” with QUB Musique.

Their follow-up single, "Talk Shit," set the tone for the EP, garnering over 8k streams in the first week and earning support from Spotify's "New Blood", and QUB Musique's "Nouveautés Musique Métal - Cover" playlists shortly after the EP's release.
